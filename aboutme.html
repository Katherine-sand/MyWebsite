About me

Hello, my name is Katherine Sandys, and I live in Noblesville, Indiana. My Family includes my Mom, Dad, and sister Lauren. I am currently a High School senior. When I am not in school and have free time, I do enjoy traveling and being active.


Scuba Diving

I have been scuba certified since the summer of 2014. I have been scuba diving in Hawaii, Cayman Islands, Curaçao, and Mexico. The coolest thing I have seen while diving is either a seahorse or a bunch of turtles. I love diving with my family; you can see a whole different world below the water.

Running

I have been running since 5th grade. My 3k time is around 11:50 and my 5k time is 19:41. I have done two 6 mile races with my sister. On May 18, I will be running my first half marathon. I am excited to see how I will do at a longer distance race.

Taking things apart

I have taken apart mostly electronics; watches, a speaker, and a 2005 desktop monitor are a few of the items I have taken entirely apart. I like taking things apart and figuring out how everything works together. I always take apart things that do not work correctly, that way I do not have to worry about putting the item back together.

Skiing

Traveling
